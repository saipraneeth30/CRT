F1 Language Overview

F1 is designed to be a powerful tool for financial analysts and developers, providing a robust framework for building applications that require high-speed calculations and data processing.

  • Built-in support for financial data types

  • Strong community support and documentation

F1 is particularly popular among quantitative analysts and developers working in the finance industry. Its ability to handle large datasets and perform complex mathematical operations makes it a valuable tool for financial institutions.
